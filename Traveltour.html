MKO Abiola Gardens and Parks- Ketu,Lagos-Nigeria

My travel tour to MKO Abiola gardens and parks at Ketu

Statue & park was dedicated to Nigeria's greatest statesman

This is a symbolic park named after the iconic legend,Chief Moshood Abiola. A politician who was widely regarded as the winner of the inconclusive 1993 Nigerian elections.

Today, the park service expresses optimism that the monument will forever crystallize what he stood for during his lifetime. In 2018, a park in Alapere,Ketu was named after him by the Lagos state government
